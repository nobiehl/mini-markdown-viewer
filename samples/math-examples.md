# Mathematical Formulas Examples

Welcome to the MarkdownViewer mathematical formula examples!

## Getting Started

You can write mathematical formulas using LaTeX syntax with KaTeX rendering.

### Inline Math

Use single dollar signs `$...$` for inline formulas:

- The famous equation: $E = mc^2$
- Pythagorean theorem: $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$
- Quadratic formula: $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$

### Display Math

Use double dollar signs `$$...$$` for display formulas (centered, larger):

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

## Common Examples

### Fractions

$$
\frac{a}{b} \quad \frac{1}{2} \quad \frac{\frac{1}{x}+1}{y-1}
$$

### Superscripts and Subscripts

$$
x^2, \quad x^{10}, \quad x_i, \quad x_{ij}
$$

### Square Roots

$$
\sqrt{2}, \quad \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}, \quad \sqrt[3]{8}
$$

### Greek Letters

Common Greek letters: $\alpha$, $\beta$, $\gamma$, $\delta$, $\theta$, $\lambda$, $\mu$, $\pi$, $\sigma$, $\phi$, $\omega$

### Summations

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$

### Integrals

$$
\int_0^1 x^2 \, dx = \frac{1}{3}
$$

### Limits

$$
\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1
$$

### Matrices

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
$$

## Real-World Examples

### Physics

Newton's second law: $F = ma$

Kinetic energy: $E_k = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$

### Statistics

Standard deviation: $\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}(x_i - \mu)^2}$

### Calculus

Derivative of $x^n$: $\frac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1}$
